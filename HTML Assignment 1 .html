Gardening Tips for Beginners

Choose Native Plants

Native plants are adapted to your local climate and require less water and maintenance. They also support local wildlife, like pollinators.

Compost for Soil Health

Create a compost bin to recycle kitchen scraps and yard waste, enriching your soil naturally without chemical fertilizers.
